Baby Rae's Banana Pancakes

Description

A lovely breakfast treat for scraunchy baby girls.

Quick to make with simple household ingredients, and will keep your demon child at bay for a few precious minutes while you wake up.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tbsp flour
  • 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • splash of milk
  • 1 tbsp oil

Steps

  1. Add the banana, egg, flour, baking powder and milk to a bowl and mix
  2. If you have a blender, blend the ingredients until a batter is formed. If you don't have a blender, mixing with a fork will work fine too
  3. Heat the oil in a large frying pan
  4. Dollop 2 teaspoons of batter into the pan per pancake and cook for 1 minutes
  5. Flip each pancake and cook for a further minute
  6. Place the cooked pancakes onto a plate and allow to cool for a minute or two so you don't burn the babay
  7. Serve to the little monster
  8. Be prepared to cook more!
