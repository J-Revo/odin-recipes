Red Eggs (Crusty Breakfast)

Description

Red Eggs, also known as Crusty Breakfast. A crusty take on the classic scrambled egg dish.

Originally curated by the lovely Hannings.

Serves 2.

Ingredients

  • 4 Eggs
  • Chorizo
  • Splash of Milk
  • 1 tbsp Butter
  • Spinach (optional, for the ultimate crusty version)
  • Salt and Pepper to season

Steps

  1. Add eggs to a bowl and whisk
  2. Add butter to a frying pan and heat slowly
  3. Chop Chorizo into small chunks and add to the heated butter, cook for 1 minute
  4. Optionally add spinach to the pan
  5. Add whisked eggs to the pan and stir continuously until eggs become solid
  6. Add salt and pepper to taste
  7. Serve with toast and enjoy
