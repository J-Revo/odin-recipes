Corned Beef and Rice

Description

A traditional dish originally curated by the beautiful Hannings.

An easy to make crowd pleaser, using ingredients available in all cupboards.

Ingredients

  • 1 Tin of Corned Beef
  • Basmati Rice
  • 1 Large Onion (sliced)
  • 1 Can Chopped Tomatoes
  • 1 tbsp Olive Oil
  • 1 tin Sweetcorn (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a dutch pot and sautee onions until translucent
  2. Add chopped tomatoes to the pot
  3. Carefully remove corned beef from the tin and chop into several large cubes
  4. Drop corned beef into the pot, optionally add sweetcorn
  5. Add your choice of seasoning, put lid on the pot and leave to simmer for 20 minutes
  6. Serve with basmati rice
